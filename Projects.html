The Exercise Diary

The following are essential exercises with their duration and importance for the purposes of a healthy lifestyle.

Crucial Exercises for a healthy lifestyle

Type of Exercise Duration Target Age Its Essence to the Body
Aerobic Exercises 3-4 hours a day Above 40 years Reduces chances of contracting obesity
Strength Training 3-4 hours a day Above 40 years Helps in management of weight and burning of calories
Stretching 3-4 hours a day Above 40 years Improves perfomnance in physical activities
Balance Exercises 3-4 hours a day Above 40 years Prevents falls and reduces the risk of lower-extremity injuries
Abdominal Crunches 3-4 hours a day Above 40 years Strenghening of the core muscles and thus improving posture
Push-ups 3-4 hours a day Above 40 years Strengthening of the lower back
